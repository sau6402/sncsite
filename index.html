About Academy

"Music fills the infinite between two souls" - Gurudeb Rabindranath Tagore

Music, an inseparable constituent of our lives, is an art form whose medium is sound and silence and comprised of tones ordered horizontally as melodies and vertically as harmonies.

The Strings & Chords is a state-of-the-art Academy of Music where we are dedicated to providing high quality and affordable training in wide spectrum of instrumental as well as vocal music.

Our highly qualified, experienced and professional instructors at the Strings & Chords will facilitate you to pursue your musical endeavours as a passion or a profession or simply as a hobby.

The salient features of Strings & Chords are:

  • Air-conditioned classrooms with vibrant ambiance
  • Coaching for all age groups
  • Individual training batches made available upon request
  • Special batches for housewives
  • Personal guidance for Trinity School of London Exams as well as any other specialized exams
  • Introducing Western Vocal training program in Thane

It is the mission of the Strings & Chords to inspire in students a life-long love of music as the fundamental art.

About Founder

The Strings & Chords Academy of Music is conceptualized and founded by Preeti Nimkar-Joshi, a young singing talent from Thane. A gifted singer, Preeti has pursued music since her early childhood years.

Preeti has showcased her singing skills in over 150 stage shows accompanying celebrated music directors Kalyanji-Anandaji and Anil Mohile in major cities across India. She has also performed in over 400 live musical shows namely Marathi Baana, Aawaaj Ki Duniya, and Aazadi50 by Ashok Hande's Chourang Production.

Preeti has recorded movie background scores, albums and serial title songs and completed singing assignments under the direction of many renowned Hindi and Marathi composers. Additionally, she has scripted and anchored 200 live episodes of musical show ‘Sa Re Ga Sare Gaauyaa’ on ‘Saam Marathi’ TV Channel. She has also launched music album named Sur Anandghan which included 2 solo and 3 duet songs written by majestic Mangesh Padgaonkar and composed by Vinay Rajwade.

Preeti has given many SOLO performances in various other TV shows noteworthy amongst them being ETV's 'Maze Jeevan Gaane' and Saam Marathi's 'Swaranjali'.

Preeti is actively encouraged and supported by her husband, Nikhil Joshi, an accomplished tabla player himself, in this venture. Both, Preeti and Nikhil, aspire to transfer their passion for music to the society by means of the Strings & Chords Academy of Music.
