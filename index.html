About Academy

"Music fills the infinite between two souls" - Gurudeb Rabindranath Tagore

Music, an inseparable constituent of our lives, is an art form whose medium is sound and silence and comprised of tones ordered horizontally as melodies and vertically as harmonies.

The Strings & Chords is a state-of-the-art Academy of Music where we are dedicated to providing high quality and affordable training in wide spectrum of instrumental as well as vocal music.

Our highly qualified, experienced and professional instructors at the Strings & Chords will facilitate you to pursue your musical endeavours as a passion or a profession or simply as a hobby.

The salient features of Strings & Chords are:

  • Air-conditioned classrooms with vibrant ambiance
  • Coaching for all age groups
  • Individual training batches made available upon request
  • Special batches for housewives
  • Personal guidance for Trinity School of London Exams as well as any other specialized exams
  • Introducing Western Vocal training program in Thane

It is the mission of the Strings & Chords to inspire in students a life-long love of music as the fundamental art.

About Founder

The Strings & Chords Academy of Music is conceptualized and founded by Preeti Nimkar-Joshi, a young singing talent from Thane. A gifted singer, Preeti has pursued music since her early childhood years.

A few highlights about Preeti:

  • Preeti Joshi holds a MASTERS DEGREE [M.A] in Classical Music from SNDT University, Mumbai
  • She is also a SANGEET VISHARAD in Classical music from Gandharva Mahavidyalaya
  • She has earned Central Government’s MUSIC SCHOLARSHIP for 21 years.
  • She has completed 8th Grade of Trinity School of London as a Western Vocalist
  • She was felicitated by Arun Date Academy as a ‘Best Female Singer’
  • As a singer and anchor, she did over 200 live Episodes on music for Saam Tv’s musical quiz show.
  • She has sung episode songs for serials like phulpakharu (zee yuva), radha hi bawari (colours)
  • She has also performed as guest singer for the musical show ‘Zing Zing zingaat’ on zee Marathi. She was also a special invitee to participate in "Aaj Kaay Special" show on colors Marathi channel.
  • Delivered 150+ live musical shows with well-known music directors Kalyanji-Anandaji and Anilji Mohile in major cities across India
  • She has shared stage with legendary singers like, Sureshji Wadkar, Anupji jalota, Avadhoot Gupte, Hrishikesh Ranade, Uttraji Kelkar, Sairam iyer and many more
  • She has recording experience with well-known Music Directors like Chinar-Mahesh, Saleel Kulkarni, Kaushal Inamdar, Vishal-Jagdish, Vinay Rajwade and many more
  • She has recorded many Rhymes and Songs for ‘Pebbles’ Company in different languages.
